Project Description

Lenticel is a Flask-based Webapp designed to convert Apple Music playlists into a Spotify playlist saved to your library. Once signed in, you may also browse through your saved playlists, and view public Spotify playlists. The converter takes a link to a public Apple Music playlist, and a user inputed playlist name and description. The program will also attempt to grab the playlist image when converting to spotify.


I made this program to solve a problem I was facing on almost a daily basis. My partner is an avid Apple Music user, while I am a Spotify user. We love to share music with eachother, and make eachother playlists. With this tool, all she has to do is send me a link to the Apple Music playlist she made, and I can save it to my Spotify Library in no time.
